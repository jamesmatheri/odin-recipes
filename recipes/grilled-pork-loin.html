Grilled Pork Loin Chops

Descrption

A great grilled pork chop marinade that's tangy, a little sweet, and very easy to prepare.
The pork loin chops turn out wonderfully tasty with an awesome array of flavors. You might want
to throw an extra chop on the grill for leftovers because you will have a craving for it the next
day — guaranteed.

Ingredients

  • ½ teaspoon ground ginger
  • ½ teaspoon onion powder
  • ¼ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ⅛ teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 2 (6 ounce) thick-cut boneless pork loin chops
