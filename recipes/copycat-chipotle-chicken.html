Copycat Chipotle Chicken

Description

This chipotle chicken recipe requires just a small amount of effort for a delicious and healthy meal.
You can use this chicken for tacos, burritos, bowls, or fajitas. Just add your favorite toppings such
as lettuce, salsa, rice, beans, cheese, or sour cream. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 2 teaspoons sea salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Place chipotle and ancho chile peppers in a shallow bowl; pour in water.
    Cover the bowl and let sit at room temperature until peppers are softened,
    10 to 12 hours. Drain water and remove seeds from peppers.
  2. Blend chile peppers, red onion, garlic, sea salt, cumin, oregano,
    and black pepper in a blender until a coarse paste forms; add olive
    oil and blend until marinade is smooth.
  3. Place chicken thighs between 2 sheets of heavy plastic on a solid, level
    surface. Firmly pound chicken with the smooth side of a meat mallet to a
    1/2-inch thickness.
  4. Place chicken in a resealable plastic bag and add marinade. Turn chicken
    several times to coat. Seal bag and marinate in the refrigerator for at least 8 hours.
